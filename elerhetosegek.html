"My name is Michael O'Reilly from Carlow (originally from Warrenpoint). For the past 10 years I have had treatment with many different dentists North and South. Because of severe gum disease which resulted in bone loss, the only option available for me was either implants or dentures. Because of the cost I could not afford to have the treatment carried out in Ireland. In 2006, I contacted dental practices in Hungary and Poland. In October 2006 I had a panoramic X-ray taken which was sent to Hungary. I had 9 implants, bone grafting, 2 quadrants periodontal surgery and temporary crowns fitted. In July, I returned and had 22 porcelain crowns fitted. The treatment I received was first class and the entire dental team make you feel welcome."
