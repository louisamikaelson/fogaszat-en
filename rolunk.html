The Perfect Smile Dental Centre, one of Hungary's largest dental clinics, awaits its patients with complex solutions to all dental and oral-surgical problems at the highest European standards.

We are specialized in asthetic dentistry and implantology. The tranquil athmosphere of our clinic combined with the undevided attention and professional expertise of our staff all ensure that you will have the most beautiful smile possible.

The quality of our work is guaranteed by our complex diagnostic system, digital panoramic X-ray, CT facility, and the highest quality equipments, implements and materials.

The dental laboratory is integrated to our clinic so our technicians are at close range to our dentists.

All our applied dental and oral-rurgical materials and products are selected from the best, most highly reputable brands and international suppliers.

Our dentists and oral surgeons are registered with not only the Hungarian, but with different European dental councils, and we are running dental clinics in Ireland and Italy.

Naturally, all our works are provided with a complete guarantee in accordance with EU standards.

Our international reputation, born from the quality and level of service, has made our clinic one of the main destinations of dental tourism in Hungary. Patients travel to us mostly from the UK, Ireland, Germany, Switzerland and Italy.

The growing number of European patients brought the idea to open our new dental clinics in Wexford and Ravenna. Our dental specialists travel regularly to these cities to offer aftercare to patients who have been treated by us in Hungary. With this international presence it's a target to get closer to our European patients. In the new postoperative care and consultation clinic in Wexford we can look after your every needs, both before and after treatment in Hungary. The clinic has two fully equipped surgeries. Recently one of the most modern digital X-ray machines was installed which can give us very accurate pictures within minutes. Our computer system is linked to our clinic in Hungary, this way we have access to all the treatment records and former X-rays, wherever we are in Europe.
